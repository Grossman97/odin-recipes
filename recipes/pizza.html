Pizza

We know you're going to love this pizza recipe!

To make this delicious pizza recipes, you only need a few common ingredients, and a quiet afternoon!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour.
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter.
  • 1/4 cupe milk.
  • 1 package of cheese.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees farenhite
  2. Combine ingredinet sin a bolw, mix, and knead for 12 minutes
  3. Allow dough to rise undisturbed for 2 hours
  4. Roll into shape.
  5. Cover with preferred toppings
  6. Bake for 20 minutes
